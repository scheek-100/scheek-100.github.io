About

Sasha Cheek is a systems integration specialist and freelance science writer, with a hunger to demystify engineering and science through storytelling.

Her career began with a systems engineering NASA internship, followed by recent technical liaison and program management roles at Microsoft and Indeed. In addition to working full-time in technology, Sasha is an M.A. candidate in Science Writing at Johns Hopkins University.

When Sasha is not debugging code and creating articles, she's dissecting the science behind her favorite films and TV shows.

Services

The services I offer cover technical consulting, mentorship and guidance for individuals in engineering and tech, integrations, and freelance science writing services.

*Improve existing technical documentation or create them to streamline information about API architecture, systems engineering, data feeds, and other similar topics.

Science & Technology Writing

*Report on science and technical expertise in narrative form as a contributor, SME, and for freelance writing.

Mentorship & Advisory
