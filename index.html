ABOUT

Sasha Cheek is a systems engineer and science writer who explores how technology, data, and human systems intersect.

Her work focuses on translating complex engineering and organizational processes into clear, data-driven stories that reveal their real-world impact.

With experience managing large-scale API and data integrations for global platforms, she brings a rare technical fluency to her writing. Sasha is currently an M.A. candidate in Science Writing at Johns Hopkins University, where she studies how to connect rigorous analysis with accessible communication.

When not building or debugging systems, she writes about the people, structures, and ideas shaping the future of science and technology.
