ABOUT

Sasha Cheek is a systems engineer who explores how software, data, and human systems intersect.

Her core career centers on systems engineering with specializations in API integrations, data, and platform integrity. She is also talented in translating complex technical ecosystems into clear, actionable insights that support real-world decision-making.

Sasha also writes about science and technology as a complementary practice, using storytelling to make complex engineering and scientific concepts accessible to general audiences. Writing enhances her technical work; it is not a separate career track.

With experience leading API and data integrations across global teams, she brings deep technical fluency to her communication. She is currently pursuing an M.A. in Science Writing at Johns Hopkins University to strengthen her ability to explain the systems and structures shaping the future of technology.
